### 2.3. Battery Technology & Flight Time Extensions (SPEAKER_02 & SPEAKER_01)
A significant portion of the discussion focused on extending drone flight time to expand the effective reconnaissance zone.

*   **Current Achievement:**
    *   **SPEAKER_02** reported successfully modifying **Mavic 3T** drones.
    *   By replacing native batteries with custom ones (14,000 mAh), flight time was extended to **72 minutes** (up to 70-80 mins in some reports).
*   **Technical Challenges:**
    *   **Voltage Mismatch:** Native batteries operate in a range of **14V to 17.6V**. The custom batteries operate starting from **12V**.
    *   **Energy Loss:** Because of the voltage difference, a portion of the energy cannot be extracted by the drone's electronics ("part of the energy from the battery cannot be taken").
    *   **Pilot Endurance:** A 72-minute flight is physically demanding for a single pilot.
*   **Operational Tactic ("Carousel"):**
    *   To maintain continuous surveillance, a "carousel" method is used: one drone lands, another takes its place immediately.
    *   **SPEAKER_01** noted that increasing flight time allows antennas to observe further (20-23 km).

> **Excerpt:**
> *   **SPEAKER_02:** "Отримали час перебування в повітрі 72 хвилини. Але це проблемний час." (We got a flight time of 72 minutes. But this is a problematic time.)
> *   **SPEAKER_02:** "Тобто частину енергії від телящої неможливо забрати." (Meaning, part of the energy from the battery cannot be taken.)
> *   **SPEAKER_01:** "Я про те, що зі збільшенням часу в повітрі, антенни зараз постерігають якраз оце 20 кілометрів, 23." (I mean that with increased time in the air, antennas now observe exactly these 20 kilometers, 23.)
